What is BiasBridge?

BiasBridge is a web-based platform for students to explore controversial political topics, examine real-time news from both sides, and engage in structured debates designed to develop empathy, reduce bias, and promote political literacy.

Platform Features

Debate Dashboard

Organize structured debates, assign moderators, and distribute students into opposing teams intelligently.

News Bias Filtering

Integrate live news feeds classified as left/right using NLP and control article visibility by debate phase.

Perception Analysis

Compare pre/post debate surveys using visual charts to track changes in opinion and self-awareness.

Feedback & Comments

Leave post-debate feedback, summaries, and peer reviews to promote thoughtful reflection and learning.

Podcast Archive

Watch recorded sessions or listen to debates on YouTube to revisit highlights and promote wider discourse.

Future Expansion

Bring debates to the full student body through a gamified battle system and school-wide voting platform.
